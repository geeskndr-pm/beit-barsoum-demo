## About

**Beit Barsoum** is a Cairo-based jewelry workshop and handmade jewelry brand, established in 1942. They offer hands-on jewelry-making workshops for beginners and enthusiasts, as well as handcrafted jewelry collections. They operate in Egypt and Qatar.

- **Price**: LE 3,400
